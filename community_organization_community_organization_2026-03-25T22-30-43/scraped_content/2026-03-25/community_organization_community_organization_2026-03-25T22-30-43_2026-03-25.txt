				<h2>Join us at Greenpoint Library and learn more about Jane's Walk NYC!</h2>
				
			</div>
		</div>
		<div class="row event-content-and-meta">
			<div class="col col_7"><p>On <strong>Thursday, March 26 from 5:30-7:00 PM</strong>, join the Jane’s Walk NYC Team at Brooklyn Public Library&#8217;s Greenpoint Branch for this in-person information session. The session will include instructions for submitting a Jane’s Walk, an overview of important dates and deadlines for the festival, and a walk topic brainstorming activity. There will be time for Q&#038;A.</p>
<p>Whether you are a first time walk leader, or you have been around the block, we invite you to join us! </p>
<p>The walk submission deadline is on <strong>Tuesday, March 31st</strong> so this session will prepare you to submit your walk. Email <strong>janeswalknyc@mas.org</strong> for any questions about the festival.</p>
<p>Jane&#8217;s Walk NYC 2026 is the weekend of May 1-3. <a href="https://www.mas.org/events/janes-walk-nyc/" target="_blank">Learn more about the festival ></a></p>
</div>

107 Norman Ave. at Leonard St.<br />
Brooklyn, NY 11222</p>								<p>Tickets:<br/>Free!</p>

        </div><div class="row text_container" id="body_0"><div class="col col_7"><p><span class="small_title_or_label">About Greenpoint Library and Environmental Education Center</a></span><br />
Greenpoint Library and Environmental Education Center serves as both a full-service library and a community hub for environmental awareness, activism and education.</p>
<p>At 15,000 square feet, the branch offers significant indoor and outdoor spaces for activities related to the exploration of the environment as well as everyday library use. Greenpoint Library features elements that interact with the natural world, including a gardened reading deck, windows that act as sun dials, and a cistern to collect rainwater for the rooftop demonstration garden. Inside, the library has distinct adult, young adult and children’s reading rooms and collection spaces, and community spaces for regular library programming as well as those supporting environmental education. Lab spaces for interactive projects, a large community event space (which divides into two of the lab spaces), lounge seating, small meeting rooms and staff spaces are distributed on the two main floors. <a href="https://www.bklynlibrary.org/locations/greenpoint" target="_blank">Learn more ></a></p>
