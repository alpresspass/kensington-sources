# NO KINGS Protest at Brooklyn Federal Courthouse

## Summary
A large crowd gathered outside the Brooklyn Federal Courthouse on Monday, March 30th, to protest against the "NO KINGS" initiative. The demonstration drew significant attention from local media outlets.
