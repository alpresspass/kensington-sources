# $4 Million Lottery Ticket Sold in Brooklyn

## Summary
A winning lottery ticket worth $4 million was sold at a store located on the corner of 8th Avenue and 59th Street in Brooklyn. The lucky winner has until May 1st to claim their prize.

## Sources
- **Brooklyn Eagle**: ["$4M lottery ticket sold in Brooklyn; winner has until May 1st to claim"](https://brooklyneagle.com/articles/4m-lottery-ticket-sold-in-brooklyn-winner-has-until-may-1st-to-claim/)

## Why It's Important
A $4 million lottery win is a significant local story that brings positive news to the community. The deadline of May 1st creates urgency for anyone who may have purchased a ticket at this location.

## Kensington Relevance
While the store is in Sunset Park (south Brooklyn), lottery stories often generate interest across all of Brooklyn as residents check their own tickets and wonder if they might be winners too.
