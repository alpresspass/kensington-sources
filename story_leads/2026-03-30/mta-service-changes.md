# MTA Service Changes Affecting North Brooklyn

## Summary
Multiple MTA bus routes serving the North Brooklyn area experienced service changes on March 30th, including:

**Routes near Kensington (11218):**
- **Q59**: Detoured from Grand St/Grand Ave between 55th St and Metropolitan Ave Bridge in both directions
- **Q54, Q59**: Williamsburg-bound buses bypassing the stop on Grand St at Graham Ave
- **B61**: Detoured from 9th St between 3rd Ave and Smith St; Northbound stop on Smith St at Atlantic Ave closed
- **B44**: Southbound stop on Nostrand Ave at Avenue N closed; Northbound detour from Bedford at Flushing Ave to Lee Ave at Wallabout St

**Other affected routes:**
- Q25, Q61: Bypassing Linden Pl/35th Ave bus stop due to construction
- Multiple Queens and Bronx routes with various service changes

## Sources
- **MTA Alerts**: Scraped from https://mta.info/alerts

## Why It's Important
These service changes directly impact daily commuters in Kensington and surrounding neighborhoods. The Q59 is a major route connecting Greenpoint, Williamsburg, and Long Island City - all areas with significant commuter traffic.

## Kensington Relevance
HIGH - These routes serve Kensington residents who commute to Manhattan, Queens, and other parts of Brooklyn. The Grand Street Bridge area (Q54/Q59) is particularly relevant as it's a key access point for Kensington residents heading to Manhattan or Queens.
